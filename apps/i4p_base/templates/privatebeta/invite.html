{% trans "Who are we ?" %}

{% blocktrans %} Imagination for People (IP) is a multilingual, Wikipedia-like open platform dedicated to disruptive social innovation. At a global scale, IP aims to detect, publicize, amplify and adapt creative solutions to the new challenges of living together. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Who is it for ?" %}

{% blocktrans %} Imagination for People gives voice to the many communities and individuals throughout the world who, with neither credit nor press, reinvent the world through tangible, exemplary and unique humanistic, social and civic undertakings. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Why IP ?" %}

{% blocktrans %} Imagination for People is explicit in its focus on the uninhibited imagination. The experiences it aims to collect and expand upon are not only socially aware or environmentally sound, they are extraordinary, disruptive, audacious, and rule-changing. No open platform dedicated to civic imagination currently exists. {% endblocktrans %}
