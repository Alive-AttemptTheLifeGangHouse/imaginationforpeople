Open Process
============

`I4P` follows an open process for its development and you are welcomed
to be part of it.

We mainly use the following tools to coordinate the development of I4P:

#. Redmine: https://dev.imaginationforpeople.org/redmine/
#. Github: https://github.com/ImaginationForPeople/imaginationforpeople/
#. Transifex: https://www.transifex.com/projects/p/imaginationforpeople/

Feel free to create an account on these tools and submit your changes!

Continuous integration
======================

To make sure we keep quality to the top, every commit is sent to Travis_.

Check our builds at http://travis-ci.org/#!/ImaginationForPeople/imaginationforpeople .
